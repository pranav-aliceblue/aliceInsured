What is a POSP (Point of Sales Person)?

A Point of Sales Person (POSP) serves as a verified, licensed intermediary connecting top insurance providers securely to prospective clients. Empowered by the IRDAI's latest Bima Sugam initiative, POSPs operate strictly within an open architecture framework, allowing them to legally distribute high-demand products like health, motor, and term life insurance from over 50+ providers simultaneously without being tied down to a single corporation.

With medical inflation driving health cover awareness and the recent 2026 policies pushing 0% GST slabs on pure health/term plans, life and medical insurance categories remain the most lucrative recurring-revenue streams available to independent agents today.
